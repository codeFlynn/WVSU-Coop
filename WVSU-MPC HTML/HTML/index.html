A little introduction...

The West Visayas State University Multi-Purpose Cooperative, formerly the Agri-Consumers’ Laboratory Cooperative started formal business operation in the main campus of the University in January 1980. A brainchild of Dr. Silva U. Hipolito, the Agri-Consumer’s Laboratory Cooperative had its founding members the seventeen (17) Agri-Business students of her Agricultural Marketing and Cooperative class in the second semester of School Year 1979-1980 and the eight (8) University faculty and staff members, mostly from the School of Agriculture. To become a full-fledged member of the Laboratory Cooperative, one was required a minimum subscription of Two shares of stock and a payment of Ten (10.00) pesos. After complying with all the paper requirements, the Agri-Consumers’ Laboratory Cooperative was given recognition by the Presidential Assistant on Community Development (PACD), Regional Office 6, in 1980.
